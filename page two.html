What is Wings of Fire about? and who are the Characters?

A war has been raging between the dragon tribes of Pyrrhia for years. According to a prophecy, five dragonets will end the bloodshed and choose a new queen. But not every dragonet wants a destiny.

The main Characters are Sunny, Starflight, Glory, Clay, and Tsunami
